Welcome to IHM Ranchi

Founded in 2019, IHM Ranchi (SIHM) is an autonomous institution under the Government of Jharkhand's Department of Tourism. Supported by the Ministry of Tourism, Government of India, we provide quality education to develop skilled hospitality professionals.
We offer opportunities to talented and enthusiastic students from Jharkhand and beyond, preparing them to become future leaders in the hospitality industry. Join us in our journey to excellence and be part of the vibrant hospitality sector.
