FAQ's

Quick answers to common academic queries about programs, admissions, and more.

1. What courses are offered at IHM Ranchi?

+

IHM Ranchi offers diploma, degree, and certificate programs in Hospitality and Hotel Management.

2. What is the duration of the courses?

+

The bachelor's degree program is for three years, while diploma courses typically last one to two years.

3. How can I apply for admission?

+

Applications can be submitted online via the NCHMCT JEE portal, followed by counseling for seat allocation.
