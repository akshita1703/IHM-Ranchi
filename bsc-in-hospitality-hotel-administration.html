Affiliation

Age Limit

No age restriction

Eligibility for Admission

A candidate must have passed 10+2 or its equivalent examination with English as one of the subjects. For Application of admission in Degree Course , NCHM JEE is conducted by National Testing Agency(NTA) through Computer Based Test(CBT)

About B.Sc in Hospitality and Hotel Administration

Degree in B.SC in Hospitality and Hotel Administration is awarded by Jawaharlal Nehru University(JNU), New Delhi under MoU with National Council For Hotel Management and Catering Technology(NCHMCT). This is a Three Year (Six Semester) full time regular course and as per NEP 2020,

also the program has 4th year option after which one get the honors.
